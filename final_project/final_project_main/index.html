Final Project: Midwest Medical Provider Landscape

Final Project

This webpage contains a few visualizations to explore the proportion of medical providers to the population in those counties as well as the percent change of medical providers in the midwest over a period of 8 years.

Small Multiples Choropleth Maps

The small multiples map shows a similar grouping of low rates of midlevel providers and physicians as well as lower median age for the population around the Minneapolis/St. Paul metropolitan area. This is not unexpected since cities tend to attract younger individuals.

Small Multiples Maps of Percent Change

The small multiples maps show the percent change of medical providers for each county in the midwest every 2 years 2015 to 2023.

Vehicle and Passenger Ferry Travel in Alaska

The small multiples maps show the percent change of physicians (MDs and DOs) for each county in the midwest every 2 years 2015 to 2023.

Choropleth Map of Iowa Medical Providers by Zipcode

The small multiples maps show the percent change of midlevel medical providers (NPs and PAs) for each county in the midwest every 2 years 2015 to 2023.
